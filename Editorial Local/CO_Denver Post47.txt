Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump has suggested that the election is rigged, both in terms of media coverage and voter fraud. Editorial cartoonist John Cole suggests Trump's "rigged" claims are nothing new, while Joe Heller depicts Supreme Court nominee Merrick Garland as incredulous at Trump's suggestion.
